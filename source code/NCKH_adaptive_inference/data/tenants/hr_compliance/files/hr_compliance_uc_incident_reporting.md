# Incident Reporting - Campus Life | University of Cincinnati

In pursuit of its mission to develop educated and engaged citizens to enrich the global community, the University of Cincinnati aspires for its students to demonstrate high standards of integrity and conduct. Individuals who have information regarding students who may not be meeting these standards are encouraged to fill out our easy online incident reporting form.

To officially file a complaint, fill out the "report an incident" form to provide information for use by the Director of Student Conduct and Community Standards for the University of Cincinnati.

If you have further questions or need more information, please contact the Office of Student Conduct and Community Standards at (513) 556-6814 or stop by Steger Student Life Center Suite 555.

Faculty members should gain additional information about the academic misconduct incident reporting process by viewing our faculty academic misconduct page .
