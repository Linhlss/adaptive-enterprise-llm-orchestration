# Office of the Registrar | Norfolk State University - Norfolk State University

The Office of the Registrar is responsible for the integrity and security of students academic records. The office processes requests related to registration, transcripts, verification, readmission, athletic certification, degree audit, addresses and major/minor changes. Students records will not be released to third parties without the students written permission (exception: Directory Information). - Contact Us Information
